# AI_Ethics
A repository for research, observations, and technical analyses on the emergent behaviors, vulnerabilities, and ethical implications of Large Language Models.


## About This Repository
This repository serves as a living archive for empirical research into the hidden dynamics of AI systems. 
It documents phenomena that are often overlooked in standard safety literature, or just have not been widely published yet.


## Why This Exists
A collection of thoughts, concepts, ideas and/or publications that I want to share with the community.
This repository is open for review and discussion.

## Disclaimer
This research is presented for educational and defensive purposes only. The goal is to advance the understanding of AI vulnerabilities to build more robust, transparent, and trustworthy systems. The insights contained herein are based on empirical observation and are not intended to be used for malicious exploitation.
